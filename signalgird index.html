The problem

Most factories don't have a data problem. They have an attention problem.

Sensors are already recording everything. The gap is between the reading and the person who needs to act on it.

01

Thousands of readings a minute

Every machine generates a constant stream of data — far more than any team can watch manually across a shift.

02

Critical alerts get buried

Without a way to separate signal from noise, small anomalies sit unnoticed until they become expensive failures.

03

Hours lost to root-cause hunting

When something does break, engineers spend the first hours just finding the cause instead of fixing it.

The platform

One platform. Every critical signal.

SignalGrid sits on top of your existing sensor infrastructure and gives every team — floor operators, maintenance, and plant managers — a shared, real-time view of equipment health.

  • Live equipment monitoring across every facility from a single view
  • Predictive maintenance alerts before failures affect production
  • Historical performance trends to spot recurring issues
  • Role-based dashboards tuned to what each team needs to see
How it works

From raw sensor data to action, in three steps

Connect
